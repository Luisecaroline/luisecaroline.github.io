reLOCATE

a furniture rental system

the idea

Do you know the problem, when you just living for a short while somewhere and you don’t have any furniture or not the right one? In times of turnovers and the need of flexibility organizing the place to stay is not easy. So a solution could be a rental system for furniture. Instead of camping in an empty room or buying cheap Ikea-furniture, which you throw away at the end, there is furniture, you just use for a certain time and you don’t need to think about where to get furniture, how to transport it or what to do with it at the end. On the other hand: How such furniture need to look like and what kind of requirements are there, which are necessary for a rental system? reLOCATE is a draft around these questions...
