about the project

All over the world the need and the possibility of mobility is increasing, but especially in big cities we have to find alternative solutions regarding problems like rush hours, traffic jams and delivery services, which are blocking the roads and other problems like the lack of parking lots. Regarding this owning a car is not advantageous even though this form of individual mobility promises a high degree of flexibility and is the expression of luxury and status. So how could a traditional car brand like Audi, who just sell cars still can be present as brand in the future? To answer this question, we analysed Shanghai, one of the biggest cities in China. Here the problems of mobility standing out more extreme, but at the same time the car as status symbol is still important, which made this city interesting.

the concept

Our solution for a mobility concept is the MoHai UNIVERSE, which can be branded to any kind of car brand. It is composed of 4 different parts: MoHail LOUNGE, MoHai NAVIGATION, MoHai STATUS and MoHai COOPERATIONS. Because of this system the MoHai MEMBERS will have exclusive access to mobility and infrastructure they need, which is more than just moving from a to b.
